Brainstorming

I chose the content package "Theorizing the Web" to create my poster as I thought my font would fit into the theme and content present in the document the best. My goal during the ideation process was to portray the message in a manner that was visually appealing and easy to read and navigate.

Inspiration

I spent some time browsing to through artwork and other typographic posters for inpiration. The first thing that drew my eyes was an image with bright oranges and pinks- I thought this colour scheme would work well within the context and I was inspired by the retro 90's color scheme which I used for my background.

I was also particulary drawn to the use of vertical text as seen in the images below.

Ideation

Combining the ideas from my inspiration, I began creating wire-frames to visualize my ideas.

My goal was to create hierarchy through the vertical and horizontal juxtaposition of lines. The left vertical heading is an overarching heading framing the two respective pages, while the hierarchy within through text sizing and colour also create additional grading. I wanted to represent the information in a concise yet visually interesting manner.

Conclusion

Overall, I learned an immense amount throughout this project. Although it was rigorous, it was a fun challenge. As someone who was not very comfortable with code, I have gained a wealth of knowledge through the process.
